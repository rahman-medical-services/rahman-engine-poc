Femoral Hernia Pathway

Because femoral hernias pass through a narrow anatomical space, they carry a significantly higher risk of trapping bowel (strangulation). Therefore, the "watch and wait" approach is not clinically recommended for femoral hernias. Prompt surgical repair is the standard of care.

Planned vs Emergency Recovery

Repairing an umbilical hernia early, while smaller, allows for a simpler, tailored day-case operation. Delaying surgery until the hernia becomes large or traps tissue dramatically increases surgical complexity and significantly extends recovery.
